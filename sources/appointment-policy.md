# Appointment Policy

## Booking Policy

- Appointments can be booked during business hours
- Same-day appointments subject to availability
- Walk-ins welcome but appointments preferred

## Cancellation Policy

- Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance
- Late cancellations may incur a fee
- No-shows may be charged a no-show fee

## Confirmation

- You will receive a confirmation via SMS or email
- Please confirm your appointment 2 hours before

## Late Arrivals

- If you are more than 15 minutes late, your appointment may need to be rescheduled
- Please call if you are running late
