# Appointment Policy

## Meeting Booking Policy

- Meetings can be booked during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM HKT)
- We accept next-day bookings from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM HKT
- Same-day meetings subject to availability
- Please book at least one day in advance when possible

## Cancellation Policy

- Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance
- Late cancellations may result in forfeiture of booking priority
- Repeated no-shows may affect future booking availability

## Confirmation

- You will receive a confirmation via email or phone
- Please confirm your attendance 2 hours before the meeting
- If you are running late, please call +852 61108487 to notify us

## Late Arrivals

- If you are more than 15 minutes late, your meeting may need to be rescheduled
- Please call if you are running late: +852 61108487

## How to Book

To book a meeting, please:
1. Call +852 61108487, or
2. Email admin@nexvision.cc with your preferred date and time

We will confirm your booking as soon as possible.
