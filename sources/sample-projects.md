# Sample Projects & Case Studies

## Project 1: Dental Clinic AI Receptionist

### Client
Multi-location dental clinic in Montreal

### Challenge
- High call volume during business hours
- Missed calls leading to lost appointments
- Staff overwhelmed with scheduling

### Solution
- AI Voice Receptionist handling 24/7 calls
- Appointment booking integrated with clinic software
- Call routing to on-call dentist for emergencies

### Results
- 95% of calls answered (vs 60% before)
- 40% reduction in missed appointments
- Staff can focus on in-clinic patients
